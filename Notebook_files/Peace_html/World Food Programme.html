World Food Programme (WFP)

Nobel Lecture

2020 Nobel Peace Prize lecture

The 2020 Nobel Peace Prize lecture will be held by David Beasley, Executive Director of the World Food Programme (WFP) on 10 December as part of the 2021 Nobel Peace Prize award ceremony at the Oslo City Hall, Norway. The ceremony starts at 13:00 CET.

The award ceremony and the lecture will be streamed live at nobelprize.org.. The Nobel Peace Prize lecture will be published here on 10 December at 13:00 CET.
